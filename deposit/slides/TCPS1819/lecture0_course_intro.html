Technologies for connected product and systems

Goals of TCPS

  • Understand and choose the hardware platform needed to build a product prototype.
  • Devise and implement a program for that platform by using a range of programming methods.

Organization of lectures

TCPS lectures/labs will be scattered around as follows:

Week Day Topic
3 Oct. 3 Recap about hwardware and software development.
Makecode platform. Hands-on work.
4 Oct. 10 Concurrent and reactive programming. Hands-on work
8 Nov. 7 Low-level programming (serial and infrared ports). Hands-on work
9 Nov. 14 Connecting to the internet. Tutoring
10 Nov. 21 Tutoring
11 Nov. 28 Tutoring

Prerequisites

  • Rudimentary knowledge of the (Arduino) hardware platform
  • Rudimentary knowledge of the (Arduino) software development process

How you will be evaluated

Group work. Hanson to be delivered through BEEP, will account for 5% of the PDS grade to be assigned by Week 8.

Individual work. As part of the whole project delivery at the end. Will be averaged with Prof. Ajovalasit and Prof. Ciancia at the end.
